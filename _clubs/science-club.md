Science Club met weekly in the lab to run experiments, build circuits, and
debate the great questions of the universe. Highlights of the year included
constructing a working model volcano, launching a model rocket on the back
field, and placing second in the regional Science Olympiad invitational.

Special congratulations to Malcolm and Stevie for their award-winning
project on water filtration!
