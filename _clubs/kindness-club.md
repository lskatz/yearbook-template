The Kindness Club believes that small acts make a big difference. This year the
club organized a winter coat drive, wrote encouraging notes to students at the
local hospital, and started a "Compliment Wall" in the main hallway that became
a fixture of daily school life.

Thank you to every member for making our school a warmer place!
